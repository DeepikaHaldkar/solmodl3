chicken

Please slice chicken in to 1” long strips Marinate with a pinch of all these ingredients salt, pepper, red chili powder, dhanya powder and turmeric for 2hrs . In a pan add 1 tablespoon oil and 1 tablespoon butter and fry the marinated chicken until 90% cooked. Once chicken is ready keep the chicken aside Please take a large deep pan, once pan is hot add oil, 1 table spoon butter Add zeera ,onions together, ginger, and garlic. Fry for 2 min Add tomatoes and cook for 5 min on medium flam stirring occasionally (let the water in tomatoes drain and look all mashed ). Let this get cool down Transfer contents into blender, keep the pan for next steps. Blend it into smooth consistency in the blender. In the same pan on LOW flame, add little oil and add red chili powder, dhanya powder, turmeric, garam masala mix it once. Contents will burn if flame is high. Pour the blended puree ( using a strainer ) into the pan. Stir it and let it simmer for 1 min, if too thick add water. Add chicken ,kastoori methi and remaining butter let it simmer for 2 mins Check salt Done Please slice chicken in to 1” long strips Marinate with a pinch of all these ingredients salt, pepper, red chili powder, dhanya powder and turmeric for 2hrs .
